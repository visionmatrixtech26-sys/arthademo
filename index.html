Who We Are

Design is transformation,
not decoration.

Artha Interior Designers is a luxury interior design studio based in Bangalore, Karnataka. Since 2024, we've built our practice on one conviction: a space should be shaped around the people who live in it, not the other way around. Every project starts as a conversation, not a catalogue.

From minimalist apartments to opulent family homes, each collaboration is bespoke and client-led — your vision, your budget, your lifestyle, translated into a space that feels entirely yours.

"Where every space tells a story."

How We Work

The Design Process

Four stages, the same on every project, so you always know what comes next.

01

Discovery Consultation

We sit down with you to understand your vision, your routines, and how you want a space to feel.

02

Concept & Mood Board

Visual direction takes shape — colour stories, material palettes, and a point of view for the space.

03

Design Development

Detailed floor plans, 3D renders, and full specifications, refined with you until every detail is right.

04

Execution & Delivery

We manage vendors and timelines end to end, so the final handover is the only surprise.
